## Progression Mechanics

### Unlocking System
1. **White Belt Stripe 1:** Always available (or after completing Foundation Modules)
2. **White Belt Stripe 2:** Unlocks after completing Stripe 1 (all 4 lessons)
3. **White Belt Stripe 3:** Unlocks after completing Stripe 2
4. **White Belt Stripe 4:** Unlocks after completing Stripe 3
5. **White Belt Assessment:** Unlocks after earning all 4 stripes
6. **Blue Belt Stripe 1:** Unlocks after passing White Belt Assessment (80%+)
7. **Pattern repeats** for Blue â†’ Purple â†’ Brown â†’ Black

### Stripe Requirements
- Complete all lessons in the stripe module (4 lessons each)
- Each lesson awards +25 XP
- Module completion awards +100 XP bonus
- Stripe badge awarded immediately on module completion

### Belt Requirements
- Earn all 4 stripes for that belt
- Pass belt assessment with 80% or higher
- Assessment includes:
  - 10-15 scenario-based questions
  - 3 reflective journaling prompts
  - 1 practical demonstration task
  - Optional peer feedback component

### Assessment Retakes
- Unlimited attempts allowed
- 7-day cooldown between attempts (configurable)
- Score must reach 80% to pass
- Previous high score displayed
