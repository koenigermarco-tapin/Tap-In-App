The 4 Fs

Fight, flight, freeze, fawn—these aren't choices. They're automatic responses wired by evolution and shaped by experience. You can't eliminate your default, but you can learn to catch it faster.

The goal isn't to never get triggered. It's to shorten the time between trigger and awareness.
