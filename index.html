Who We Are & What We Do

Who We Are

We're a group of people who want to help others achieve more, in every aspect. Our founders bring real-world leadership experience from scaling teams through extreme challenges.

Flo has led a business and worked with over 700 companies through workshops and team development programs. Marco was Director of Operations in a tourism startup with 100 people (responsible for 60), and before that scaled a local delivery hub from 0 to 2,000 orders per day, then managed 2,500 couriers across 10 cities with 100 staff—at age 28. He led teams through hypergrowth during COVID with 350-450% year-over-year growth while staff only grew 50%.

Tap-In comes from this real experience. The anonymized data and insights from managing thousands of people through crisis and growth inform every tool we build.

What We Do

This app is just one way we show how we could improve working together. We create practical, gamified leadership development tools based on real experience managing teams through hypergrowth, crisis, and everyday challenges.

Our assessments reveal your leadership DNA. Our games build real skills. Our tools solve real problems. All designed to fit into your schedule, not replace it.

How We're Bringing It to You

We want to bring this to people—not just through an app, but through community and real-world experiences.

Skool Community: Join our community where leaders share experiences, learn together, and support each other's growth.

Coming to You: We're building programs to bring leadership development directly to your organization.

Real Jiu-Jitsu Training: Our long-term vision includes combining leadership development with actual Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu training—because leadership, like Jiu-Jitsu, is best learned through practice, not just theory.
