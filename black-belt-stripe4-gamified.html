The multiplier mindset.

Every interaction is a choice: will you add value, or multiply it?

Adder vs Multiplier

Adders
Solve problems Problems stay solved
Share knowledge Knowledge is transferred
Lead a project Project succeeds

Multipliers
Teach problem-solving Problems get solved forever
Develop teachers Knowledge spreads exponentially
Grow leaders Projects multiply

Black Belts think in multiplication, not addition.

The Succession Moment

You're being promoted. Your replacement is competent but not outstanding. What do you do with your last 2 weeks?

Create detailed documentation of everything you do
Invest intensively in developing your successor's judgment
Tie up loose ends and finish outstanding projects
Introduce them to key relationships
Judgment Over Information
Documentation captures what you did. Development creates capacity for what they'll face that you can't anticipate. Black Belt legacy is about judgment transfer, not information transfer.
