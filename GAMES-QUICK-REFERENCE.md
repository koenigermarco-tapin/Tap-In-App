## ğŸ¯ DISAGREE & COMMIT

**Players**: 3-6  
**Time**: 30-40 min  
**Theme**: Decision-Making (Purple Belt)

**How to Play**:
1. Read business scenario with pro/con arguments
2. Think silently (30 seconds)
3. Debate as team (60 seconds)
4. Vote: Agree or Disagree
5. Unanimous = +2 pts each, Consensus after 2nd debate = +1 pt
6. Can use BLOCK token (3 per player) to skip

**URL**: `/disagree-commit.html`  
**Win Condition**: Highest score after 10 scenarios

## ğŸ’¼ CONFLICT CARDS

**Players**: 4-10  
**Time**: 30-45 min  
**Theme**: Feedback & Conflict (Blue Belt)

**How to Play**:
1. Judge reads black card (conflict scenario)
2. Others submit white card responses (pass device)
3. Judge picks best response
4. If winner used SBIR feedback = 2 pts, otherwise 1 pt
5. Rotate judge, first to 7 points wins

**URL**: `/conflict-cards.html`  
**Win Condition**: First to 7 points
